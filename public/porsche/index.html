Pure. Unfiltered. Track-bred.

The 911 GT3 RS is the closest road car to a Porsche racing machine. Every aerodynamic surface, every gram of weight, every degree of camber — engineered with one purpose: to make you faster.

With its naturally aspirated 4.0-litre flat-six engine revving to 9,000 rpm, the GT3 RS delivers an experience that modern turbocharged cars simply cannot replicate. This is motorsport, distilled.

Every curve, every vent, every surface is sculpted by aerodynamics. The swan-neck rear wing generates up to 860 kg of downforce at 285 km/h. The front splitter, side blades, and rear diffuser work in concert, creating a ground-effect car for the road.
