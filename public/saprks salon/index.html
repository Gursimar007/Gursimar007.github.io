At Sparks Salon, we believe beauty is not just about appearance — it's about how you feel. Nestled in the heart of Chandigarh, our salon is a sanctuary where luxury, creativity, and expertise converge to craft truly transformative experiences.

Led by seasoned professionals, our team stays at the forefront of global beauty trends. From precision haircuts to radiant skin treatments, every service is delivered with meticulous attention to detail and a passion for perfection.

We use only premium, certified products — including our trusted Wella Professional range — ensuring your hair and skin receive nothing but the very best care.
